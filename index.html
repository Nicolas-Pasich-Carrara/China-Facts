Facts about China

China is a big country in the middle of Asia. It is the 4th largest country in the world and it has the biggest population of any country in the world, about 1,443,251,911 people live in China. About 50 million Chinese live outside of China. About 60,00 Chinese live in Ireland.

China has the worlds tallest mountain range in the world. almost 200 of the world's tallest mountains are in the himalayas including mount Everest, the tallest mountain in the world that lies 8848 metres above sea level.

The Tempature in China can vary from being very hot, to being very cold. In The summers, the average tempature is 25 c, but it can go up to as high as 40 c. In the winters, The average tempature is 0 c, But it can go to as low as -30 c.

China's Climate varyies from being very hot and arid to being very cold. In the north near Mongolia it is mild and arid with a few deserts and grasslands around. In the southeast, rainforests dominate most of the area with 2 major rivers flowing through them, the Yellow river and the Yangteze river. In the southwest, It is very mountinous, cold and icy. In the northeast, It is very cold during Winter, and very hot during summer. In the east the climate is hot and wet. the airflow from the sea generally brings in rain and monsoons.

China slso has a lot of money. According to the world bank, China has the largest economy in the world, totalling to 90 trillion Yuan, or 11,610,985,833,000 euro.

China has 14 land borders. They are Vietnam, Khazakstan, Mongolia, Russia, North Korea, India, Nepal, Bhutan, Kyrgyzstan, Laos, Myanmar, Pakistan, Tajikistan and Afghaninstan. It has maritime borders with Brunei, Indonesia, Japan, South Korea, Malaysia, the Philippines, and Taiwan.

China has A very big military. With 2.8 million troops, It has the most troops in any army, about double of the USA, witch is in 2nd place. It has about 600 planes, 537 naval ships, 3,500 tanks and 30,000 armoured vehicles.
